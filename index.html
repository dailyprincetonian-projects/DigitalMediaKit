What is the ‘Prince’?
The Daily Princetonian is Princeton University’s leading daily publication. Founded in 1876, the ‘Prince’ is student-run and operates independently of the University. Though our focus has traditionally been on the print newspaper, we’ve wholeheartedly embraced online journalism, with over 1.5 million site visits in the first half of 2020. We’ve gained a larger audience through social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. During school, we email a daily newsletter to nearly three thousand subscribers. The ‘Prince’ strives to achieve high journalistic standards and drive on-campus dialogue about pressing issues.

*Due to COVID-19, ‘The Prince’ is not printing this summer. During the upcoming school year, ‘The Prince’ will mail a monthly copy to all students and other Princeton community members. Interested parties should contact the business@dailyprincetonian.com for details.
